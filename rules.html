Rules of the Game

It really is like the classic game of Battleship: a turn-based, two-player, strategy-like (but mostly chance) guessing game.

Before the game begins, you'll place your bubbles of each size (5, 4, 3, and 2) on your board. You can rotate them vertically or horizontally and place them however you like— so long as they don't overlap each other or fall off the board! When you're satisfied with how you've strategically (or aesthetically) placed your bubbles, the game will begin!

On your turn, you check your opponent's map and make a guess at where your opponent's bubbles might be hidden! Just point and click to make your guess. (You can only guess once per turn, so choose wisely!) We'll then let you know whether your guess was a "hit" or a "miss"!

Then it'll be your opponent's turn. And they'll do the same thing! They'll look at their map of your board and make a guess, and we'll let you both know whether their guess was a hit or miss, too!

It'll be pretty clear which is which: Hits will be colored red, and misses will be solid black! Don't worry, we'll keep track of both for you, and you can't choose the same spot twice. ;) Whew!

Whoever pops all— 14, total!— of their opponent's bubbles first, wins!
